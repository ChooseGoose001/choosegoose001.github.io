Courses

Our sessions are planned to guide visually intuitive research, led through specific drawing tasks.

We will start our inaugural studio-based and online courses in June. There is more information on the dates, course description, price and how to apply below.

We want to enable an informal but focused atmosphere and so each course has a maximum of 12 participants working with 2 tutors.

Revisiting Basics 1a:

Studio based course

Monday 21 June - Thursday 24 June 2021
4 day course - 10am -4pm (Lunchbreak 1-2pm)

Our inaugural studio-based course - intended to free up and extend your range of drawing, through 4 days of drawing together, looking and discussion.

Revisiting Basics 1b:

Online course

Monday 28 June - Thursday 1 July 2021
4 day course - 10am - 4pm (Lunchbreak 1-2pm)

The online version of our inaugural course - with tasks specifically designed for online platforms, we would like to open up the course for less local participants.
