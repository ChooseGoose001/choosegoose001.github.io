COVID PRECAUTIONS

COVID SAFETY

Eye to Pencil is committed to following COVID Government Guidelines and so that we are able to maintain face-to-face teaching this summer and to play out part to keep our community safe.

We ask our participants to undertake the following precautions before you start the course with us.

Please undertake a non-symptom (asymptomatic) test as close to the start date as possible, however, informing us of the result is optional and not a requirement.

We have a thermometer to take everyone's temperature.

Please use the Track and Trace App.

Please do not attend the Eye to Pencil if you are feeling unwell, displaying symptoms or have been in contact with someone who has tested positive.

Self-isolate responsibly if you have been in contact with someone who has a Covid-positive test result.

If you test positive, self-isolate for the recommended period from the date of the test result.

Please wear a face mask at all times when inside the building.

Please sanitize your hands every time you enter the studio.

We will continue to follow social distancing.

We will have disinfectant and wipes so everyone can keep their areas/ materials and tools clean.

Please bring your own drawing materials (in case you forget something you will be able to borrow materials).

Your work areas will be spaced apart and we will ask participants stay in their area as much as possible.
