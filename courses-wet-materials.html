Course Description

This course complements the Dry Materials course, but this time over the 5 sessions we will explore a range of wet drawing materials in combination with different papers. Using various inks and watercolour we will work with line and wash, staining, opacity, layering and collaging. Using the paper itself as a part of the tonal range and for luminosity is another aspect of painting on paper we will focus on. Again we will consider the usual use of materials and work with and against these expectations.

Who

We have designed this course for students from different disciplines who are already drawing or who feel that drawing could become a useful part of their thinking process. This course is suitable to students of all ages (aged 18+) and levels of experience.

More information on this course will be added shortly. Please contact us if you have any questions.
