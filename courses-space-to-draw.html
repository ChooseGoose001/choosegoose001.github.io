A Space to Draw

A Space to Draw – Mondays, hourly, All Day

Every Monday, starting 5 July, 2021

1 hour - 7 hours
Mondays, 10 am – 6 pm

Hourly: £8 if you use Studio materials, £6 if you bring your own materials.


Course Description

Every Monday we are opening up our studio to offer you a space to draw on an hourly basis. This is a chance to develop your own work in an unguided way, draw from a still life we will have set up or draw whatever you like. Come and join us to draw together.

We could open the studio for longer hours if there is the demand, so get in touch if you would like to draw in the Drawing Studio beyond the current hours.

Who

These sessions are suitable for anyone (aged 18+) with any level of experience, it is an informal and open session for anyone wanting to draw.

Materials and Useful Info

There will be basic studio materials available to use (paper, pencils, charcoal, indian ink, erasers) at the £8 per hourly rate however feel free to bring your own sketchbook or specific materials if you prefer, for £6 per hour.
