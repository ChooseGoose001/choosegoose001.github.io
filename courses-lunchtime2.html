LUNCHTIME DRAWING - In the Park

LUNCHTIME DRAWING – Wednesday Lunchtime

7, 14, 21 and 28 July, 2021

1 hour
Wednesdays 1 – 2 pm

£8 (including paper, and the loan of a drawing board, pencil, eraser)
Easy payment by card reader on site.

No Booking necessary - Come and find us in Spa Fields, it will be first come, first served.


COURSE DESCRIPTION

We are hoping for sunny days for lunchtime drawing in Spa Fields EC1, just around the corner from the Drawing Studio. We will bring pencils, drawing boards and paper. Come and join us to draw whatever you like. We will be there for informal feedback, suggestions and supervision for those who want it.

WHO

These sessions are suitable for anyone (aged 18+) with any level of experience, it is an informal and open session for anyone wanting to draw.

MATERIALS AND USEFUL INFORMATION

We will bring paper, and will lend pencils, erasers and drawing boards.
