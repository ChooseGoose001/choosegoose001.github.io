Eye to Pencil

is a drawing project with an interdisciplinary approach, exploring how drawing is part of our thinking process and connects us to a visual world.

 

Courses

Our inaugural courses will be starting in June '21 in the studio and we will also be running an online version of the course. More details and info on how to apply, or follow us on Instagram @eyetopencil for updates.


Talks

We will be running a series of talks with experienced creatives across a range of disciplines, engaging in discussions about common visual ground and where drawing fits in.


Studio

Our drawing studio will be a space to draw together and a platform for visual exchange.

It is located in the centre of Clerkenwell, London.
