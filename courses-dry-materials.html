Course Description

Over the 5 sessions we will explore a range of dry drawing materials in combination with different papers. Through using different materials, and combinations, we will become more sensitive to the feel and potential of the materials at hand. As well as working with mark making tools (eg. pencils, chalk, charcoal, conté) we will constantly be aware of the importance of erasure, building an image up and when necessary paring it back again. We will consider the usual use of materials, particularly within specific fields or contexts, and also the challenges and surprises of misusing and experimenting with them. This course is complemented by the following Wet Materials course.

Who

We have designed this course for students from different disciplines who are already drawing or who feel that drawing could become a useful part of their thinking process. This course is suitable to students of all ages (aged 18+) and levels of experience.
