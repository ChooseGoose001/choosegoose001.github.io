Transcription

Transcription - Tuesday Evenings, Studio

2nd & 4th Tuesday of each month, starting 13 July, 2021

2 hours
Tuesday 6.30 - 8.30pm

Â£24


Course Description

These fortnightly sessions begin with Antje and Luci talking about one artwork, using our screen and projector. Looking from the similar but different perspectives of art historian and painter, and we would like to draw in other voices as the discussion develops. The second half of the session will be a short drawing exercise to consider a specific aspect of the artwork and the visual language it uses, and to warm us up for the next task which is to create a longer slower transcription of the artwork. These exercises are aimed at understanding the work further, and focusing on what about it attracts or interests you.

Who

Transcribing through drawing is often a regular activiy for artists, however it can also be a great introduction to drawing and is suitable for all ages (aged 18+) and all levels of experience. We would like to encourage lapsed drawers who want to get back into it to join our fortnightly evening sessions.

Materials and Useful Info

There will be basic studio materials available to use (paper, pencils, charcoal, erasers) however feel free to bring your own sketchbook or specific materials if you prefer.
