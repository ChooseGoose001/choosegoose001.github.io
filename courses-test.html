Courses

Our sessions are planned to guide visually intuitive research, led through specific drawing tasks.

Our inaugural courses start in June. As we get Eye to Pencil up and running we will trial a variety of courses and timings. We welcome feedback and queries, so get in touch if you have questions. We will be adjusting our courses for 2022 with this feedback in mind. There is more information on the dates, course description, prices and how to apply below.

We want to enable an informal but focused atmosphere and so each course has a maximum of 12 participants working with 2 tutors.

Revisiting Basics 1a:  

Studio based course

21 - 24 June 2021
4 day course - 10am - 4pm (Lunchbreak 1-2pm)

Our inaugural studio-based course - intended to free up and extend your range of drawing, through 4 days of drawing together, looking and discussion.


Revisiting Basics 1b:  

Online course

Dry Materials 2a:  

Studio based course

14 Sept - 12 Oct 2021
1 morning per week for 5 weeks - 10am - 1pm

Over the 5 sessions we will explore a range of dry drawing materials in combination with different papers. Through the process becoming more sensitive to the feel and potential of the materials at hand.
